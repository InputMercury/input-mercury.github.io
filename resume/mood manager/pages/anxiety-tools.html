Splash Cold Water On Face

Splashing cold water on your face can reset your system, which can instantly make you feel calmer. Additionally, you can run cold water on your wrists, and this will have the same effet.

PMR

PMR stands for progressive muscle relaxation, this is when you tighten your muscles in each group as tightly as you can, and then release slowly. This releases muscle tension and helps with relaxation

Controlled Breathing

Controlled breating, or sometimes called box breathing, is when you inhale as deeply as you can into your abdomen for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, release for 4 seconds, and then again hold for 4 seconds.

Visualization

This method involves imagining a place whether it be from a happy memory, or an imaginary place that you have created, picture that image in your mind and immerse yourself in the emotion that you feel when you think of that place.

Compassionate Self-Talk

Anxiety tends to come from a place of self-criticism. Believe in yourself, and reassure yourself that you have the skills to cope.
